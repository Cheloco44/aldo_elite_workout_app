THE WESTSIDE CONJUGATE SYSTEM

Louie Simmons' elite powerlifting system — the same methodology that produced more world record holders than any other gym on earth. You train strength and speed simultaneously, every week.

  • Max Effort (ME): Work up to a 1–3 rep max on a variation. Rotate exercises weekly to avoid accommodation
  • Dynamic Effort (DE): Move submaximal weight with maximal intent — bar speed is the metric
  • 4 days per week: ME Lower, ME Upper, DE Lower, DE Upper
  • Accommodating resistance (bands/chains) on DE lifts adds strength curve overload
  • Rotate ME exercises every 1–3 weeks — never adapt, always attack
