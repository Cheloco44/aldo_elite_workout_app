🕊️ The Templar's Covenant — Bible Diet

"He heals the brokenhearted and binds up their wounds." — Psalm 147:3

The body is the temple. What you consume is an act of devotion. The Templar eats not merely for performance, but for purity — fueling the vessel God gave you with food grown close to creation.

⏱️ Fasting Protocols

Try each for two weeks. Track fasting times, meals, energy, mood, and physical changes. Drink water, herbal tea, black coffee, veggie/fruit juice.

12/12 — The Initiate's Fast

12 hours eating, 12 hours fasted. Begin here. Build the discipline before the discipline builds you.

18/6 — The Sergeant's Discipline

18 hours fasted, 6-hour eating window. The body shifts to fat as fuel. Mental clarity sharpens.

20/4 — The Knight's Trial

One major meal and one small meal. Intense focus. Train fasted if possible — this is combat readiness.

24 Hours — The Grand Master's Fast

Once or twice per month. One day of purging. Allow one hour of all-you-can-eat foods once or twice a week.

🍵 Healing Leaves Tea

Mulberry, Kiwi, Kaffir Lime, Grape, Guava, Moringa, Fig Leaves. Top 5 trees: Mango, Olive, Soursop, Raspberry, Avocado.

Fill 2½ quarts water. Add 3 tbsp fresh or 1 tbsp dried leaves from each tree. Boil, simmer 12 min. Cover 20 min. Strain. Consume 16–32 oz per day at room temp or cold. Add honey, maple syrup, stevia, or monkfruit to taste.

🧂 Celtic Salt Protocol

Use Celtic salt or unrefined salt only. Add a pinch to every glass of water to increase hydration, reduce blood pressure, aid digestion, and improve nutrient absorption.
