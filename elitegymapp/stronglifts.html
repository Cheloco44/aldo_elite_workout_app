THE STRONGLIFTS 5×5 SYSTEM

The no-nonsense beginner blueprint that builds real-world strength fast. Two workouts. Five lifts. Add weight every single session until you're moving serious iron.

  • Workout A: Squat, Bench Press, Barbell Row
  • Workout B: Squat, Overhead Press, Deadlift
  • Alternate A/B, 3x per week with rest days between
  • Add 5 lbs per session on upper body, 10 lbs on lower
  • Deload by 10% if you miss reps 3 sessions in a row
  • 12 weeks will take most lifters from beginner to intermediate territory
