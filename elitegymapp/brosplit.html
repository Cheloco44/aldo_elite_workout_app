THE BRO SPLIT

One muscle group. One day. Maximum destruction. The OG bodybuilding split that built generation after generation of jacked athletes. Don't let anyone tell you it doesn't work.

  • 5 days on, 2 days off — Chest, Back, Shoulders, Arms, Legs
  • Each muscle trained once per week with full dedication and volume
  • High sets, moderate-to-high reps — the pump IS the program
  • Progressive overload: add weight or reps each week
  • Best suited for intermediate lifters focused on aesthetics and size
