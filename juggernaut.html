THE JUGGERNAUT METHOD

Built by CJ Murphy, this is the wave-based powerbuilding system for athletes who want to get massive AND strong. It runs in 4-week waves: Accumulation → Intensification → Realization → Deload.

  • Wave 1: 10s — Build work capacity and muscle
  • Wave 2: 8s — Transition to heavier loading
  • Wave 3: 5s — Pure strength building
  • Wave 4: 3s — Peak intensity before testing
  • Each week within a wave: Accumulation (60%), Intensification (70%), Realization (AMRAP at top %), Deload
  • Biggest lifts: Squat, Bench, Deadlift, OHP
