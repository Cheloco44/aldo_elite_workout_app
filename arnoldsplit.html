THE ARNOLD SPLIT

Six days. Two muscle groups per day. The exact framework Arnold Schwarzenegger used to build the most dominant physique in bodybuilding history. High volume, high frequency, maximum size.

  • Day 1 & 4: Chest + Back — highest volume, antagonist superset style
  • Day 2 & 5: Shoulders + Arms — isolate and pump
  • Day 3 & 6: Legs — quads, hamstrings, calves get their own spotlight
  • Each muscle group trained twice per week at different rep ranges
  • Goal: maximum hypertrophy through volume, not just intensity
