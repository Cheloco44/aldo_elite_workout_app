THE WESTSIDE CONJUGATE SYSTEM

Louie Simmons' elite powerlifting system — the same methodology that produced more world record holders than any other gym on earth. You train strength and speed simultaneously, every single week.

  • Max Effort (ME): Work up to a true 1–3RM on a variation each session — rotate exercises weekly to avoid accommodation
  • Dynamic Effort (DE): 50–60% of your 1RM moved with maximal bar speed — velocity is the metric, not load
  • 4 days per week: ME Lower, ME Upper, DE Lower, DE Upper
  • DE percentages wave in a 3-week cycle: 50% → 55% → 60%, then reset
  • Accessory work targets weaknesses: posterior chain, lockout, shoulders, core
